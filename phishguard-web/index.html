🚔 Digital Arrest Scam Detector

India's #1 cyber crime in 2025–26. Scammers impersonate CBI, ED, NCB, or Police and claim you are under "digital arrest" — forcing you to stay on video call while transferring money.

📊 ₹1,200 Cr lost in 2024
📊 70,000+ cases in 2024
📊 Avg victim loss: ₹2.1 Lakh
📊 PM Modi warned nationally Oct 2024

Analyze Suspicious Call / Message

Paste the exact words the caller said, a WhatsApp message, or an email threatening arrest.

How Digital Arrest Scams Work

1Caller claims to be CBI / ED / NCB / Police / TRAI
2Says your Aadhaar/SIM/bank account linked to crime
3Demands you stay on video call — "digital arrest"
4Pressures you to transfer money to "clear your name"
!REMEMBER: No real agency conducts arrests over video call

🛡️ What To Do If You Receive This Call

✅ Hang up immediately
✅ Do NOT transfer any money
✅ Do NOT share OTP or bank details
✅ Call 1930 (Cyber Crime Helpline)
✅ Report at cybercrime.gov.in
❌ No agency arrests via video call
❌ No court sends WhatsApp summons
❌ No officer asks for money to "clear" you

🚩 Classic Fake Job Red Flags

❌ Asks for registration/kit/training fee
❌ Salary too high for zero experience
❌ Interview on WhatsApp or Telegram only
❌ No office address or landline number
❌ Offer letter before any interview
❌ Gmail/Yahoo address instead of company email
❌ Work from home with guaranteed ₹50k/day
❌ Asks for Aadhaar/PAN before joining

📌 How To Spot Fake Forwards

❌ "Forward to 10 people or bad luck"
❌ No source, no date, no author
❌ Govt scheme asking for personal details
❌ "Breaking news" with no media outlet
❌ Emotional language designed to trigger fear/anger
❌ Links to .xyz .tk .ml domains
✅ Verify at pib.gov.in/factcheck
✅ Check on newschecker.in or boomlive.in
